## 🚨 Why This Website Exists

Students in Kathora Naka and surrounding Amravati district lacked a **single trustworthy digital presence** for their nearest government-recognized IT education center. Before this website:

- 📵 No online presence — students relied solely on word-of-mouth
- 📋 No way to enquire or apply for courses online
- ❓ No clarity on which MKCL courses are offered locally
- 📍 No map or directions for students from nearby villages

**This website solves all of that — and looks world-class doing it.**
