Admission

How to Enroll

Simple 4-step process to join Padmashri Computer and start your learning journey.

  • 1
    Online / Walk-in Application
    Fill the registration form online or visit our center directly. Our counselors will help you choose the right course.
  • 2
    Document Verification
    Submit required documents: Aadhaar Card, 10th Marksheet, Passport Size Photo. Verification is quick and hassle-free.
  • 3
    Fee Payment
    Pay the course fee through cash, UPI, or bank transfer. Installment options are available for eligible students.
  • 4
    Start Learning
    Receive your study materials, ERA access, join your batch, and start your journey toward a digital career with expert mentors.
